How It Works

1

Connect to Server

WebSocket connection established with MediaSoup SFU server. Ready to start streaming.

2

Access Camera

Request permission to access your camera and microphone. Your local video will appear.

3

Create Transport

Establish WebRTC transport connection to send your video stream to the server.

4

Start Publishing

Your video stream is now being sent to the MediaSoup server for distribution.

5

Consume Stream

Receive and display the video stream from the server. You'll see your own video as "remote".

About MediaSoup

MediaSoup is a WebRTC SFU (Selective Forwarding Unit) that efficiently routes media streams between peers without transcoding, making it perfect for real-time video applications.
